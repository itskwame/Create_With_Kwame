Agent Basics

What is an AI agent?

An AI agent is a focused digital assistant built around a specific task or process.

It can follow instructions, use approved tools, organize information, draft responses, summarize work, route requests, and support your team with human oversight.

It is not magic. It is not a replacement for leadership. It is a smarter way to support repeated work.
