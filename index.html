The Reality

Most people are already using AI. They just are not using it on purpose.

AI is already built into phones, GPS, autocorrect, search, streaming, social media, customer service, shopping apps, and workplace tools. Consuming AI is not the same as being prepared for it.

The Difference

Tools Change. Thinking Lasts.

Most AI training teaches people how to use the tools that exist today. I teach the foundations, judgment, and critical thinking skills people need to succeed with any tool, model, or upgrade.

Tool-Based Training

  • Follow features
  • Learn the interface
  • Copy prompts
  • Outdated quickly
VS

Thinking-Based Training

  • Ask better questions
  • Check outputs
  • Apply judgment
  • Adapt to any tool
  • Use AI correctly and responsibly
