The Difference

Tools Change. Thinking Lasts.

Most AI training teaches people how to use the tools that exist today. The problem is that tools, models, and features change constantly. I teach the foundations, judgment, and critical thinking skills people need to succeed with any tool, model, or upgrade.

Tool-Based Training

  • Follow features
  • Learn the interface
  • Copy prompts
  • Outdated quickly
VS

Thinking-Based Training

  • Ask better questions
  • Check outputs
  • Apply judgment
  • Adapt to any tool
  • Use AI correctly and responsibly
